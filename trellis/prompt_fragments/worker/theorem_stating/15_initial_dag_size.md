## Initial theorem-DAG size guidance

When theorem-stating is in its initial broad build-out stage with no held target yet, a good target for the initial proof-bearing DAG size is `{{theorem_initial_dag_size_guidance}}` proof-bearing nodes (definition nodes don't count towards this target), depending on the paper's complexity.

This is guidance, not a legality rule. Follow the paper's real structure: do not pad the DAG gratuitously, but also do not collapse a clearly multi-step argument into a small trivial package.
