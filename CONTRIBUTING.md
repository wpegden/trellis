# Contributing

Trellis is released under a source-available noncommercial license (PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0). Commercial rights are reserved by the copyright holder.

External pull requests will not be reviewed without prior discussion. Please open an issue describing your proposed change first.

Small issue reports, bug descriptions, and high-level suggestions are welcome without prior coordination.

Unless separately agreed in writing, accepted contributions are made available under the same license terms as the rest of the repository.
